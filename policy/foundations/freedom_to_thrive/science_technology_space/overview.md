# Science, Technology, and Space — Overview

*This file is a stub. Narrative content is pending.*

Scientific leadership, public research infrastructure, and responsible stewardship of space are national imperatives. This pillar covers federal R&D funding floors, open-access research publishing, STEM education investment, the democratic accountability of emerging technologies, and governance of commercial and government space activity — including debris accountability and the democratic oversight of space-based systems.
