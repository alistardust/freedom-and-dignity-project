## Core Principle
Wealth concentration must not undermine democracy or economic fairness.

## Purpose
Define how wealth is taxed, distributed, and prevented from accumulating into political dominance.

## Key Areas (To Be Fully Developed)
- Progressive taxation requirements
- High marginal rates for top earners (e.g. 70% discussion)
- Wealth taxation frameworks
- Georgism / land value taxation
- Anti-hoarding mechanisms
- Closing tax loopholes and avoidance strategies
- Corporate taxation reform
- Alignment between tax system and democratic stability

## Open Questions
- What should be constitutional vs statutory?
- How to enforce without capital flight or avoidance?
- How to measure and cap harmful concentration?
