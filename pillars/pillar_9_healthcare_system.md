## Core Principle
Healthcare must be treated as a guaranteed right, not a privilege.

## Purpose
Define how healthcare is delivered, funded, and protected from political interference.

## Key Areas (To Be Fully Developed)
- Universal healthcare structure
- Funding models
- Coverage guarantees
- Anti-politicization protections
- Regulation of insurers and providers
- Access standards across geography
- Protection of medical decision-making autonomy

## Open Questions
- Single-payer vs hybrid systems?
- Federal vs state administration?
- How to enforce equal access?

## Status
Integrated into rights, needs standalone expansion.
