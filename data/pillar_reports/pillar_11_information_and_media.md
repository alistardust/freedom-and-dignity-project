## Core Principle
Information systems must not be weaponized to manipulate the public or undermine democracy.

## Purpose
Balance free speech with protections against coordinated deception, foreign influence, and systemic manipulation.

## Key Areas (To Be Fully Developed)

- Regulation of AI-generated content
- Transparency requirements for media funding and incentives
- Political advertising disclosure
- Platform accountability
- Civil remedies for harmful misinformation campaigns

## Open Questions

- Where is the line between opinion and manipulation?
- How to avoid abuse of these powers by government?
