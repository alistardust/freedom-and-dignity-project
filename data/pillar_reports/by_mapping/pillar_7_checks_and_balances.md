## Core Principle
No branch of government should be able to operate without meaningful oversight or accountability.

## Purpose
Reinforce separation of powers and create enforceable mechanisms when one branch abuses or exceeds its authority.

## Key Areas (To Be Fully Developed)
- Impeachment reform:
  - expanded criteria
  - clear definitions of misconduct
- Conviction mechanisms:
  - alternative thresholds or dual systems
- Congressional oversight power expansion
- Enforcement mechanisms when executive refuses compliance
- Citizen-triggered oversight or impeachment concepts
- Structural guardrails for institutional abuse
- Strengthening legislative authority relative to executive expansion

## Open Questions

- How to prevent abuse of oversight mechanisms themselves?
- Should citizens have formal triggering power?
