Foundation II: Clean Democracy

Information & Media

★ ★ ★

A functioning democracy requires a free, diverse, and trustworthy press.

Why This Matters

Local news has collapsed. National media is consolidated into a few corporate hands. Social media algorithms amplify outrage and disinformation because engagement is profitable. The information environment required for democratic self-governance has been systematically destroyed.

What It Demands

  • Federal tax credits for local news organizations structured as nonprofits or cooperatives
  • Mandatory algorithmic audits for platforms with more than 10 million users
  • Prohibit news outlet ownership by private equity firms and foreign-controlled entities

What It Rejects

  • Platform self-regulation as sufficient response to disinformation
  • Media consolidation that leaves communities without local reporting
  • Algorithms optimized for engagement at the expense of accuracy and social cohesion

Key Policy Points

  1. Local news funding
  2. Platform transparency requirements
  3. Algorithmic accountability
