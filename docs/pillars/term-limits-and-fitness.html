Foundation I: Accountable Power

Term Limits & Fitness

★ ★ ★

Elected and appointed officials must be term-limited and meet basic fitness requirements.

Why This Matters

Lifetime tenure without accountability creates a class of officials who are answerable to no one. At every level of federal government, there must be mechanisms to ensure fitness and limit entrenchment.

What It Demands

  • 12-year term limits for members of Congress
  • Mandatory cognitive and health disclosure for all federal offices with national security responsibilities
  • Retirement age of 75 for federal judges, including Supreme Court justices

What It Rejects

  • Lifetime Supreme Court appointments as compatible with democratic accountability
  • Career politicians insulated from electoral consequences by safe seats and incumbency
  • Officials with documented cognitive decline holding positions of national authority

Key Policy Points

  1. Congressional term limits
  2. Cognitive and fitness assessments for federal office
  3. Mandatory retirement for federal judges
