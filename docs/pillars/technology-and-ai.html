Foundation IV: Real Freedom

Technology & AI

★ ★ ★

Technology must serve people. Surveillance capitalism and unaccountable AI are threats to freedom.

Why This Matters

The most comprehensive surveillance apparatus in human history was built in the last twenty-five years, almost entirely by private companies, operating without meaningful democratic oversight. AI systems now make consequential decisions about employment, credit, housing, and criminal justice — with no accountability.

What It Demands

  • Comprehensive federal data privacy law: data minimization, right to deletion, no sale without explicit consent
  • Mandatory impact assessments for AI systems used in hiring, lending, criminal justice, and healthcare
  • Break up Big Tech platforms that have used acquisitions to eliminate competition

What It Rejects

  • Surveillance capitalism as a legitimate business model
  • AI systems deployed at scale without transparency or accountability
  • Self-regulation by the companies with the most to gain from the absence of rules

Key Policy Points

  1. Federal data privacy law
  2. AI accountability framework
  3. Antitrust action against Big Tech
