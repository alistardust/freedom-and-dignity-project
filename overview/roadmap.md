# Strategic Roadmap

This roadmap is reconstructed from the strategy branch discussions and kept intentionally high level.

## Main workstreams

1. **Core system** - finish and normalize all pillar documents
2. **Narrative layer** - refine the public-facing explanation and identity
3. **Infrastructure** - maintain the repo, data catalog, and future website structure
4. **Communication strategy** - create audience-aware messaging and publication plans
5. **Coalition strategy** - identify overlap with aligned movements and organizations
6. **Feedback loop** - test language, structure, and clarity before wider release

## Practical sequencing

1. Finish the pillar corpus and integrate missing tracked items.
2. Consolidate system rules and domain-level rule usage.
3. Build the structured policy catalog for tracking and later website use.
4. Expand public-facing materials once the policy architecture is stable.
