## Freedom to Thrive

> **"You can't be free if you're sick, homeless, or in debt."**

Originated in this project as a synthesis of the American democratic tradition on material freedom. Not attributed to a single source — claim it as ours.

**Lineage:**
- FDR, Second Bill of Rights speech (January 11, 1944): *"True individual freedom cannot exist without economic security and independence."* / *"Necessitous men are not free men."*
- Bernie Sanders (2019): *"Are you truly free if you are unable to go to a doctor when you are sick? … if you cannot afford the prescription drugs you need? … if you are forced to work 60 or 70 hours a week because you need to pay off your student debt?"*

**Use:** Opening line for the Freedom to Thrive foundation statement; epigraph for healthcare, taxation, and economic security pillar documents.
